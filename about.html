About

Built so no one walks in circles on day one.

CampusCoordinator is a small project that grew out of orientation week at Mae Fah Luang University. The campus is big, beautiful, and easy to get lost in — and the official map didn't really help us figure out where the canteen actually was.

So we made this: a clean, fast directory of every building, library, dorm, sports facility, and student service worth knowing. Each entry is written in plain language, with the kinds of tips you usually only learn from a senior.

The whole thing is open to the MFU community. If you spot something wrong or missing, tell us in person — that's still the most reliable channel on this campus.
